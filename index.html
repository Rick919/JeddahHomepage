Visit Jeddah

Jeddah is a unique blend of history, culture, and modernity,
offering a wide range of activities and sites to explore.
Whether you're interested in heritage, art, shopping, or relaxation by the sea, Jeddah has something for everyone.

Jeddah, located on the western coast of Saudi Arabia along the Red Sea, is a vibrant port city known for its rich history, cultural significance, and modern attractions. It serves as a gateway for pilgrims heading to Mecca and is recognized for its unique blend of traditional and contemporary architecture.

Key Highlight Of Jeddah

Historical Significance

Jeddah has a rich history as a trading hub and a major point for pilgrims. The historic district, Al-Balad, is a UNESCO World Heritage site featuring traditional coral houses and ancient souks.

Corniche

The Jeddah Corniche is a picturesque waterfront area featuring parks, sculptures, and walking paths, perfect for leisurely strolls with beautiful views of the Red Sea.

King Fahd Fountain

One of the tallest fountains in the world, it shoots water up to 1,024 feet and is a stunning sight, especially at night when illuminated.

Shopping

Jeddah offers diverse shopping experiences, from traditional markets like Souk Al-Alawi to modern malls like Mall of Arabia and Red Sea Mall.

Cultural Sites

Visit the King Abdulaziz Museum, showcasing the history of the Kingdom, and the Jeddah Sculpture Museum, an open-air gallery of contemporary art.

Beaches and Water Activities

The Red Sea is perfect for diving, snorkeling, and other water sports, with several public and private beaches available.

Cuisine

Enjoy a variety of culinary experiences, from street food to fine dining, featuring traditional Saudi dishes and international cuisine.

Things To Do

Explore Al-Balad:
Wander through the narrow streets of this historical district, admiring the traditional architecture and visiting local shops.
Visit the King Fahd Fountain
Enjoy the evening light show and take photos of this iconic landmark.
Relax at the Corniche:
Spend time in the parks, enjoy a picnic, or take a bike ride along the coast.
Shop at Local Markets:
Experience the vibrant atmosphere of the souks, where you can find spices, textiles, and handicrafts.
Dive in the Red Sea:
Take a diving tour to explore coral reefs and marine life.
Visit Museums
Discover the history and culture of Saudi Arabia at the King Abdulaziz Museum and the Jeddah Sculpture Museum.
Enjoy Local Cuisine
Try traditional dishes such as Kabsa or fresh seafood at local restaurants.
